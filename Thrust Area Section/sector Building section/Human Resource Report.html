.............................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................................

ARAVALI provides and facilitates HR recruitment in the partner organisation based on their request. The HR recruitment support is mainly for recruitment of professionals in the organisation. For this process, ARAVALI with representatives of the partner organisations initiates and finalises dates with rural management institutions for campus placement interface with students and conducts interview. The feedback of interview is shared with partners along with tentative candidates. Upon the consent of the organisation ARAVALI establishes further dialogue with the management institution and the select students and facilitates all further negotiations. ARAVALI also provides support in selection process of invited candidates by the partner NGOs. It facilitates the interview process and final selection of the candidate with consent from the partner organisations
